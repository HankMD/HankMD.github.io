Rookie Road

Manager of Automation

Rookie Road is an online website which teaches the rules, lingo, and gameplay of various sports in an easy to understand way.

Working at Rookie Road gave me a taste of the fast-paced world of technology startups. As a company that focuses on content creation, Rookie Road's technology team started small with just two people.

I began by building out a library of useful scripts and programs. As automation proved more and more valuable to Rookie Road, the chief product officer and I designed and developed the continuous integration pipeline that persistently deploys the site.
