Ahold Delhaize

Emerging Technologies Developer (Co-op)

Ahold Delhaize is one of the largest retail conglomerates in the world, with over 6000 grocery stores and multiple e-commerce grocery platforms.

I worked in the Innovation Lab locate in Quincy, Massachusetts. As a part of the Emerging Technologies, my work focused on Internet of Things solutions for deployment in brick-and-mortar stores. This involved API and backend programming in Python, computer vision programming in Python, data analytics and visualization in R and Python, and microcontroller programming in Arduino and C.

While working at Ahold Delhaize my team and I invented an automated checkout system that used customer device data. I am filed as an original inventor on the patent.
